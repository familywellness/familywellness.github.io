Free 30-Min Consultation

You’ll start with a no-strings-attached free consultation. You can choose whether to schedule coaching sessions or not after this consultation.

Book consultation here
During the consultation, you’ll:
  • Meet the coach.
  • Discuss your goal with the coach.
  • Receive a pricing quote.
  • Recieve a suggested term length to ensure adequate strides towards your goal.

    Terms are either 3 months or 5 months. The coach will suggest an appropriate term length for your specific goal. Some topics, such as parenting, will require a minimum of 5 months , while smaller projects are managable with the shorter term.

  • If you decide you want coaching sessions, you’ll pick a time slot. Secure your slot when you pay the first month’s fee.

Coaching

Recieve personalized help from the coach in each coaching session. There will be 2 coaching sessions a month. Payment is due the beginning of each month.

Here’s what to expect:

(Note that your experience will vary according to your situation and your goal)

  • Discuss your progress.
  • Coach analyzes the situation and prompts your next action steps.
